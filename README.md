## The Features That Actually Matter

### Time Tracking Without the Nonsense

Employees clock in, clock out. That's it. The system auto-calculates their hours, handles overtime, and accounts for breaks. You can set it to automatically round punches to the nearest 5 or 15 minutes if that's your thing.

If someone forgets to clock out (because let's be real, everyone does), you can set automatic punch-outs after their scheduled shift ends. No more "oops I worked 47 hours because I forgot to punch out."

### Scheduling That Doesn't Suck

The Pro and Enterprise plans come with built-in scheduling. You're dragging and dropping shifts onto a calendar, not wrestling with Excel. Employees can see their schedules on their phones, request time off, and even trade shifts with coworkers (with manager approval, of course).

The system sends automatic notifications when schedules are published or changed, so nobody can claim they "didn't know" about their shift.

### Time-Off Tracking on Autopilot

Buddy Punch handles PTO accruals automatically. You set the rules (like "2 weeks vacation per year"), and it calculates how much time everyone has available. Employees submit requests through the app, managers approve or deny with one click, and approved time off automatically shows up in timesheets for payroll.

Here's where Buddy Punch really shines. It integrates directly with major payroll providersâ€”QuickBooks, ADP, Paychex, Gusto, and more. When it's time to run payroll, you're exporting timesheet data with a few clicks instead of manually entering hours for each employee.
